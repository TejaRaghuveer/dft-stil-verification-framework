# Knowledge Transfer Plan

## Training Schedule

1. Manufacturing/Test Team Kickoff (90 min)
2. ATE Program Walkthrough (120 min)
3. Failure Debug and Escalation Workshop (90 min)
4. Post-silicon Correlation Review Session (60 min)

## Enablement Assets

- Recorded walkthroughs (video and deck)
- Lab-ready runbooks
- FAQ and troubleshooting matrix
- Mentorship pairing list for first silicon bring-up

## Q&A and Ongoing Support

- Weekly office hours during first 8 weeks of silicon bring-up
- Shared issue tracker for cross-team visibility
- Escalation SLA:
  - Critical blocker: <4 hours
  - High severity: <1 business day
  - Normal issues: <3 business days
